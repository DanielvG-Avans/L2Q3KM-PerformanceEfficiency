# Paper

Use this folder for the final research manuscript and all supporting assets.

## Structure

- `source/`: Editable manuscript source files, such as `.docx`, `.tex`, or `.md`.
- `figures/`: Charts, diagrams, screenshots, and other visuals used in the paper.
- `references/`: Citation and bibliography files, such as `.bib`.
- `final/`: Exported submission files, usually the final PDF.
