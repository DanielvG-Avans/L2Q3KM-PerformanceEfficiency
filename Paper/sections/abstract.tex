\begin{abstract}
I investigated what the influence was of server-side rendering (SSR) and client-side rendering (CSR) on the energy consumption of mobile devices. Although these render strategies are often compared based on speed and user experience, less attention is paid to resource efficiency and energy consumption. In this study, one web application was developed in two variants (SSR and CSR), where CPU and memory usage were measured under controlled conditions during the render process. The results were analyzed to gain insight into the differences between both strategies and their impact on resource efficiency. This research thereby contributes to making more informed choices around render strategies in mobile front-end development.
\end{abstract}
